About

From 2021-2024, I undertook the Graduate Diploma in Applied Statistics with the University of New England, Australia. During this course, I completed a remote sensing research project in which I used a 36-year Landsat NDVI time series to investigate the response of vegetation to ecological disturbances in the Blue Mountains National Park, NSW. Since graduating from this course, I have developed my skills in remote sensing for land cover mapping and change detection through volunteer work with Birdlife Australia. I am currently pursuing a career as a freelance data analyst specialising in remote sensing and geospatial analysis.
